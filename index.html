Wallabag 2 pocket converter

If by any chance you feel like moving from Wallabag to pocket, you've come to the right place!! This tool will help you to export your reading list from Wallabag and transform them to a format that can be imported in pocket.

Here's what you need to do :

  1. Go to your wallabag instance and click on the export button. This will download a .json file.
  2. Copy the content of the .json file you just downloaded and paste it here :
  4. Copy the content from the area (just after) and save it to an .html file :
  5. Go to your pocket import page and load the .html file you just created.
  6. Enjoy your articles on pocket...
